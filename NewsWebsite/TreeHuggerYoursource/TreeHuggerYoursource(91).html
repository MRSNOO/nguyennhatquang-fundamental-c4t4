Living Cleaning & Organizing How to read laundry care labels Melissa Breyer MelissaBreyer March 9, 2019 © Santi S Help your clothes last longer by learning how to decode the mysterious hieroglyphics of care labels.Whenever it's time to clean a new garment, I head for the care label and hope that it offers instructions in words ... and in words of a language I understand. Otherwise, I am lost. What does a crown with three dots in it mean? What's that scary black circle in a box? What does that patterned shape that looks like it belongs on an IQ test represent?I mean, I know it's great that we are offered all kinds of information on how to best care for a garment. It is important – the better care we take of an article of clothing the longer the item will last, and the better it is for our wallets and the planet.
